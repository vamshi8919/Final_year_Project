About GREEN HAVEN

Welcome to GREEN HAVEN

At GREEN HAVEN, we are passionate about bringing nature closer to you. Our nursery offers a wide variety of plants, including fruit plants, vegetable plants, commercial crops, and beautiful garden plants. Whether you're a seasoned gardener or just starting, we have the perfect plants to enrich your space.

Our mission is to promote sustainable and eco-friendly gardening by providing high-quality plants and expert guidance. We believe in the power of greenery to transform lives and make the world a better place.

Join us in creating a greener tomorrow with GREEN HAVEN!
