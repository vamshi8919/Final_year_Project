Papaya Plant Uses

Papaya is a widely cultivated fruit crop that has many uses, including in food, medicine, and industry.

Food

  • Table Fruit: Ripe papaya is eaten fresh.
  • Beverages: Papaya puree is used in fruit drinks, smoothies, and milkshakes.
  • Jams, Jellies, and Marmalades: Papaya is used in processed foods like jams, jellies, and marmalades.
  • Cakes, Puddings, and Desserts: Papaya is used in cakes, puddings, and desserts.

Medicine

  • Papain: A proteolytic enzyme used in pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and meat tenderization.
  • Papaya Leaf: Used in tea, juice, or extract, known to help with dengue fever.

Industry

  • Leather: Used in the leather industry.
  • Textile: Used in the textile industry.
  • Chewing Gum: Papain is used in manufacturing chewing gum.
  • Paper: Papain is used in the paper industry.
  • Sewage Treatment: Papain is used in sewage treatment.

Common Diseases

  • Papaya Ringspot: Causes yellowing leaves, vein clearing, leaf mottling, and ring spots on fruit.
  • Papaya Mosaic: Leads to blister-like patches, reduced leaf size, and elongated fruits with water-soaked lesions.
  • Papaya Leaf Curl: Results in downward curling of leaves, twisted petioles, and stunting.
  • Foot Rot: Causes water-soaked patches on the stem near ground level, making plants prone to falling.
  • Powdery Mildew: Leads to white or grayish powdery growth on the plant, reducing crop yields.

Pesticides/Fertilizers

Insecticides

  • Imidacloprid: A neonicotinoid insecticide.
  • Acephate: An organophosphate insecticide.
  • Chlorpyriphos: An organophosphate insecticide.
  • Duduthrin: Used to control papaya mealybug.
  • Actara: An insecticide for papaya mealybug.
  • Engeo: A pesticide for papaya mealybug.
  • Dynamec 18EC: A pesticide for papaya mealybug.

Fungicides

  • Thiophanate Methyl: A thiourea fungicide.
  • Mancozeb: A sulfur fungicide.
  • Carbendazim: Controls papaya diseases.
  • Kavach: A fungicide for disease control.
  • Roko: A fungicide for powdery mildew.
  • Contaf Plus: A fungicide for powdery mildew.

Biopesticides

  • Neem Oil: Used with isopropyl alcohol or paraffin oil.
  • Citrus Oil: Used to control papaya pests.
  • Kaybee Mealy Raze: A biopesticide for mealybugs.
  • Other pesticides: Azoxystrobin, Propiconazole, Difenoconazole, Spirotetramat, and Methyl Demeton.
