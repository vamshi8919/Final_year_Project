Paddy Plant Uses

Paddy, or rice, has many uses, including food, fuel, and animal feed.

Food

  • Flaked rice: Used to make a variety of foods.
  • Sake: A Japanese alcoholic beverage made from fermented rice.
  • Rice flour: Used to make noodles and breakfast cereals.
  • Brown rice: A whole grain that can help with weight management and digestion.

Fuel

  • Paddy husk: Used as fuel and in brick making.
  • Rice hulls: Used as fuel, packing material, and in fertilizer manufacturing.

Animal Feed

  • Bran: Used as cattle feed.
  • Rice polish: A finely powdered bran and starch used as livestock feed.

Other Uses

  • Straw: Used as livestock bedding, for thatching roofs, and for making mats, garments, and broomstraws.
  • Bran oil: Used as an edible oil, in the textile industry, and in the leather industry.
  • Bran wax: Used in the chocolate and lipstick industries.

Rice is a staple food for more than half of the world's population. It's also important to millions of small farmers and the people who work on their farms.

Common Diseases

Paddy crops are susceptible to many diseases caused by bacteria, viruses, and fungi. These diseases can reduce yields and the quality of the grain.

Common Diseases of Paddy Crops

  • Sheath blight: A disease that causes white, cottony growth on the leaves and leaf sheaths, which eventually turn brown and die. It can also cause lesions on the stem and empty grains.
  • Blast disease: A disease that causes blast-like lesions on the leaves, stem, and grains. It spreads quickly and can cause huge losses if not treated promptly.
  • Brown spot: A disease that causes small dark brown spots on the leaves. It can affect the yield and milling quality of the grain.
  • Sheath rot: A disease that reduces grain yield by retarding or aborting panicle emergence.
  • Bacterial leaf blight: A disease that is considered a major threat to rice production because of its wide spread and destructiveness.
  • Stem rot: A disease that causes premature death and lodging of the plants.
  • Tungro disease: A disease that is one of the most damaging and destructive diseases of rice in South and Southeast Asia.
